## Sponsor the Project

Sponsors are vital to the long-term sustainability of this project. Your financial support enables core development, infrastructure costs, community events, and governance operations.

By becoming a sponsor, you directly contribute to the health and growth of the ecosystem. In return, we offer recognition, visibility, and opportunities for deeper collaboration.

All sponsors are publicly acknowledged unless they choose to remain anonymous. Sponsorship funds are managed transparently through our fiscal host or governance treasury.
