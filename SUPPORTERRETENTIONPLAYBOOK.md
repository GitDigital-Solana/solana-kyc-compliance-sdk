Supporter Retention Playbook — RickCreator87 Ecosystem

A structured approach to keeping supporters engaged and valued.

---

1. Early Engagement (First 7 Days)
- Deliver badge  
- Send welcome packet  
- Provide dashboard link  
- Offer spotlight opportunity  

---

2. Monthly Touchpoints
- Newsletter  
- Release highlights  
- Governance updates  
- Creative surfa- Sees badge wall  
3. Quarterly Touchpoints
- Analytics report  
- Personalized thank‑you  
- Tier review  
- Optional call for feedback  

---

4. Annual Touchpoints
- Certificate refresh  
- Year‑in‑review  
- Recognition in annual report  

---

5. High‑Value Supporter Strategy
For Ecosystem & Institutional tiers:
- Priority updates  
- Early access to governance modules  
- Personalized outreach  
- Optional collaboration  

---

6. At‑Risk Supporter Strategy
If no engagement for 90+ days:
- Send a warm check‑in  
- Share recent releases  
- Offer a spotlight  
- Reaffirm mission alignment
