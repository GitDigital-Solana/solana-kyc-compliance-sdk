`markdown

Sponsorship Hero’s Journey

A narrative arc describing the supporter’s path through the ecosystem.

---

1. The Ordinary World
The supporter begins outside the ecosystem.

2. The Call to Alignment
They encounter the mission of transparency.

3. Crossing the Threshold
They join a tier and receive their badge.

4. Trials of Engagement
They explore dashboards, updates, and creative surfaces.

5. Allies & Mentors
They meet other supporters, ambassadors, and curators.

6. The Revelation
They understand the deeper purpose of public‑good infrastructure.

7. The Transformation
They become a long‑term supporter.

8. The Legacy Return
They enter the Legacy Circle and inspire new supporters.
`
