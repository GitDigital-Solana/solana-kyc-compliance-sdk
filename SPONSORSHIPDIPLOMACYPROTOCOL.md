Sponsorship Diplomacy Protocol

A structured approach for external engagement.

---

1. Principles
- Transparency  
- Respect  
- Mission alignment  
- Non‑influenceable governance  

---

2. Diplomatic Roles

Ambassador
Represents the ecosystem externally.

Envoy
Handles cross‑community communication.

Liaison
Coordinates collaborative creative or technical efforts.

---

3. Engagement Types
- Cross‑repo collaborations  
- Creative exchanges  
- Governance discussions  
- Public‑good partnerships  

---

4. Boundaries
- No sponsorship influence  
- No governance concessions  
- No private data sharing  
`
