Membership and Subscriptions

Membership is a way to deepen your involvement with the project while supporting its sustainability. Membership is distinct from sponsorship in that it is typically individual, recurring, and focused on community participation rather than corporate partnership.

Subscription Overview

· Individual Membership: Recurring monthly or annual contribution starting at $5.
· Supporter Subscription: Recurring contribution at any level, with acknowledgment on the support wall.
· Member Benefits: Depending on tier, members may receive recognition, voting rights in certain governance decisions, and access to member-only spaces.

Membership funds are used for operational costs, community grants, and infrastructure. All members are recognized in the membership leaderboard unless they opt out.
