Sponsorship Policy — RickCreator87 Ecosystem

This ecosystem is built on transparency, governance alignment, and public‑good principles.  
Sponsorships help sustain development but do not influence governance, roadmap decisions, or compliance standards.

Principles
- Sponsorship is voluntary and appreciated.
- Sponsors receive recognition and tier‑based benefits.
- Sponsorship does not grant decision‑making authority.
- All governance decisions follow documented processes.
- No sponsor may request privileged access to user data, private repositories, or compliance systems.

Transparency
- All sponsorship tiers and benefits are public.
- Supporter Wall updates are automated and auditable.
- Financial influence is intentionally decoupled from governance.

Independence
This ecosystem remains founder‑led and mission‑driven.  
Sponsorship strengthens the mission — it does not steer it.
