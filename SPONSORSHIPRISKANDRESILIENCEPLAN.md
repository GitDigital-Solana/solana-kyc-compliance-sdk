Sponsorship Risk & Resilience Plan

A governance‑aligned plan to ensure long‑term sustainability.

---

1. Financial Risks
- Declining MRR  
- Tier concentration  
- High churn  

Mitigation
- Diversify tiers  
- Strengthen loyalty program  
- Quarterly forecasting  

---

2. Operational Risks
- Automation failures  
- API downtime  
- Data inconsistencies  

Mitigation
- Redundant scripts  
- Manual override procedures  
- Quarterly audits  

---

3. Governance Risks
- Misaligned incentives  
- Sponsor influence attempts  

Mitigation
- Strict governance charter  
- Transparent decision‑making  
- Public documentation  

---

4. Community Risks
- Low engagement  
- Supporter fatigue  

Mitigation
- Monthly touchpoints  
- Creative artifacts  
- Recognition cycles
