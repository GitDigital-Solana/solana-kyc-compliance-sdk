What is Supportship?

Supportship is the cultural foundation of our community. It means showing up, contributing, and caring for the project and its people—whether through code, documentation, design, governance, mentorship, financial backing, or simply being a respectful and engaged participant.

Supportship is not defined by a single action or level of contribution. It is a mindset of collective ownership and mutual responsibility.

Everyone who contributes to the project in any meaningful way is considered a supporter. We celebrate supportship in all its forms.
