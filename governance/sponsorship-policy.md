# Sponsorship Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the terms, benefits, and expectations for sponsors of GitDigital-Solana. It aligns with our commitment to transparency, modularity, and governance-aligned infrastructure.

## Sponsorship Types
- **Monthly Recurring**: Provides sustained funding and governance benefits.
- **One-Time**: Provides one-time support with permanent recognition.

## Governance Rights
- **Guardian Tier**: One vote in off-chain governance forums.
- **Architect Tier**: Two hours/month of technical consultation.
- **Foundation Partner**: Veto power on critical security changes via multi-sig.

## Refund Policy
Sponsorships are non-refundable. If a sponsor cancels their monthly sponsorship, benefits are revoked at the end of the billing cycle.

## Badge Authority
All sponsors receive a badge in the `/badge-authority/sponsor-badges.json` registry. Badges are revocable if a sponsor violates the Code of Conduct or fails to adhere to sponsorship terms.

## Data Privacy
Sponsor data (username, tier, start date) is stored in `/registry/sponsors.json` for transparency. No private financial data is stored.

## Review
This policy is reviewed quarterly by the Foundation Partners.
