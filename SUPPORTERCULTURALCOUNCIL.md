Supporter Cultural Council — Concept

A supporter‑run council dedicated to cultural, creative, and community initiatives.

---

🎯 Purpose
To enrich the cultural identity of the ecosystem through:
- creative projects  
- community events  
- storytelling  
- recognition initiatives  

---

🧩 Council Roles

Curator
Oversees creative canon and artifacts.

Archivist
Maintains historical records and legacy editions.

Host
Runs seasonal events and ceremonies.

Chronicler
Documents community stories and milestones.

---

🛑 Governance Boundary
The Cultural Council has no influence over:
- governance  
- roadmap  
- compliance  
- technical decisions  

It is purely cultural.

---

🏅 Recognition
Council members receive:
- Cultural Council badge  
- Annual recognition  
- Creative spotlight
