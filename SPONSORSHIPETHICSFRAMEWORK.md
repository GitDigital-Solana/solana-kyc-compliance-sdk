Sponsorship Ethics Framework — RickCreator87 Ecosystem

A governance‑aligned ethical framework guiding all sponsorship interactions.

---

1. Independence
Sponsorship never influences:
- governance  
- roadmap  
- compliance decisions  

---

2. Transparency
- Public tiers  
- Public benefits  
- Public recognition  
- Public governance  

---

3. Fairness
- Equal recognition within tiers  
- No preferential treatment  
- No hidden benefits  

---

4. Privacy
- Minimal data collection  
- No sensitive data  
- No third‑party sharing  

---

5. Integrity
- No pay‑to‑play  
- No influence buying  
- No back‑channel access  
`
